Black Panther

Scientific Name: Panthera pardus or Panthera onca

Type of cat

A black panther is the melanistic color variant of any big cat species. Black panthers in Asia and Africa are leopards, and those in the Americas are black jaguars.

Size

An Adult Black Panther grows up to 7-8 feet in length and weighs between 100-250 pounds.

What They Eat

The black panther feeds off of warhogs, wild boar, and deer.
